Principal Investigator

Z. Morley Mao

Z. Morley Mao is a Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at University of Michigan. She received her B.S., M.S., and Ph.D degree all from University of California at Berkeley. She is a recipient of the NSF CAREER Award, Sloan Fellowship, and the IBM Faculty Partnership Award. She has been named the Morris Wellman Faculty Development Professor.
