Who We Are

Welcome to UMich RobustNet Group! Led by Prof. Z. Morley Mao, we focus on computer networks, ML systems security, efficient ML systems, and autonomous vehicles.

Research Focus

Computer Network

Measurement, routing, and telemetry that keep large networks predictable under stress.

MLSys Security

Defenses for data, models, and runtime stacks to resist poisoning and adversarial misuse.

Efficient MLSys

Scheduling, compression, and co-design to deliver fast, cost-aware ML across edge and cloud.

Autonomous Vehicle

Perception, control, and V2X integration that keep connected mobility resilient and verifiable.
